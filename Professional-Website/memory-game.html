In this screenshot i captured in a very important moment of my project: the development of a Memory Game based on JavaScript. This screenshot shows the starting point for potential players. This simple yet fun challenge has users finding pairs of matching squares distributed across the game board and clicking them together as they press on within this game. The basis of memorizing and looking at the pattern means every square will yield an icon or image with the click of its participant, and the result must be memorized to complete the remaining part of the game.

This is a screenshot of what the user will see once they have fully finished the game. They will get a good job message and can reload the page to restart.

This game helps me demonstrate the basics of combining html, css, and javascript to make a neat looking game that all users of all ages can enjoy and play.
